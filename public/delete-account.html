Account & data deletion

Delete your account and data

Oceans Symphony stores most of your data — journals, check-ins, fronting log, locations, reminders, custom statuses, and everything else you log — only on your device. A small profile is stored on our server only if you opt in to the Friends feature. You can delete either or both at any time. Nothing is retained.

Step 1 — Delete everything (in-app)

  1. Open Oceans Symphony.
  2. Go to Settings → Data Storage.
  3. Tap "Delete All Local Data".
  4. Type delete my data to confirm.

This wipes the entire on-device database and tells our server to delete your Friends profile (if you ever set one up). The server entry is removed immediately; nothing is retained after the request completes.

Step 2 — Delete only your Friends profile

If you'd like to keep your on-device journal but remove the small profile we store on our server (display name, system name, current fronter status, push subscription):

  1. Open Oceans Symphony.
  2. Go to the Friends page.
  3. Tap the settings (gear) icon → "Delete Profile".

Your local data stays intact; your friend code stops working immediately and any friends you had will no longer see your status.

Step 3 — If you can't open the app

Email pesturedrawing@gmail.com with your friend code (the 8-character code shown on the Friends page when you had the app installed). We'll remove the server-side Friends profile manually.

Note: on-device data lives only inside the app's storage on your phone. Once the app is uninstalled, that data is gone with it — we have no copy and can't retrieve or delete it from our side. Re-installing gives you a clean start.

What gets deleted vs. kept

Deleted immediately

  • All on-device data: journals, check-ins, fronting sessions, locations, reminders, alters, custom statuses, settings, integration tokens (PluralKit, SimplyPlural), encrypted vault if enabled.
  • Friends profile on our server: display name, system name, custom terms, fronter status, friend code, privacy settings, per-friend visibility overrides.
  • Push notification subscription tied to your Friends profile.
  • Pending friend requests sent to or from your account.

Kept (because we never have it)

  • Email address — we don't collect one.
  • Real name, phone number, address — we don't collect these.
  • Payment info — the app is free; there is no billing system.
  • Analytics, crash logs, or device identifiers — none are collected.

Retention period after deletion

None. Deletion is immediate. We do not retain backups or archives of deleted profiles. Server logs (Vercel access logs) may retain the IP address of the deletion request for up to 30 days as part of standard infrastructure logging — they are not associated with your account after deletion and are not used for any purpose other than abuse prevention.
